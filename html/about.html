What is Kitty Corner?

Kitty Corner is a blog dedicated to cats. Cats are a central part in many peoples lives and and especially on the internet. So it wouldn't hurt with another cat blog out there would it? This cat blog is for you who love to read about cats, learn about cats or wanting to figure out if you want a cat. We have a variety of posts about cats, from what to look for in a cat to which kind of food they enjoy the most. I hope you have a cat-tastic day!

Who is behind Kitty Corner?

My name is Bjedne and I am the creator of Kitty Corner. I am a cat lover and I have a little kitty of my own named Lulu. My family has always owned at least 1 cat at all times, so I grew up living with a cat nearby. I have always loved cats and I have always wanted to have a cat blog. I hope you enjoy my blog and that you learn something new about cats.
